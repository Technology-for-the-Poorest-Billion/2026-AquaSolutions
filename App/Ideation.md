# Mobile Phone Application for Water Quality Management

## Prototype: Web-App
- System architecture should be holistically completed, including communication between phones, sensors, data storage facility, etc.
- User Interface: Adapts to one of Zimbabwe's 16 official languages. Very simple, non-technical interface designed for minimal friction in the collection/evaluation of drinking water.
- UI/UX Design should be grounded in conversations with Mr.Chafa or residents of Harare.

## System Level
- The application layer is at the very top of the software/hardware designed for this project. It's operation is grounded in the success of the algorithms, sensors, and communications design.

## Operation
- Based on data collected at a regular frequency in the borehole, local civilians can check the app to see whether or not the water is immediately drinkable or rquires some sort of treatment.
- The app can recommend a variety of possible treatments, ranging from boiling water to chlorine tablets to the shutting off of the borehole (severe cases).
- When a contamination is detected, a technician/official is notified so that they can take the appropriate action to mitigate the risk of a bacterial outbreak.
